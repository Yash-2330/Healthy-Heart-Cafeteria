About Us

Our vision is to provide food full of nutrition. We first started our small outlet named Smiling Hearts in the city of Nagpur in the year 2006. We have been totally committed to provide the best quality, fresh and nutritous food since then. And we hope to keep going on for the years to come. We thank our customers for the love they have given us and for the blessings they have showered upon us for without their love and passion for our food, we are nothing.
